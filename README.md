# Oyster_Game
A game about being an oyster in the Chesapeake Bay

🌊 Chesapeake Oyster Reef Interactive Game

Chesapeake Oyster Reef is a simple interactive game that puts you in charge of a growing oyster reef in the Chesapeake Bay.

As oysters filter plankton from the water, they clean the Bay, build reefs, and help support healthier ecosystems. In the game, oysters automatically collect food and pollution that drifts into their filtering zone. Clean water helps oysters grow faster and form new reefs, while dirty water and human-caused pollution can damage oysters and slow growth.

Over time, human activities such as wastewater discharge, factories, and road salt increase pollution pressure. If the reef is too small or poorly maintained, water quality declines and predators can overwhelm the reef. A healthy, well-managed reef is more resilient and better able to withstand these stresses.

How to play

Place and grow oysters to expand your reef

Improve oyster filtering to keep the water clean

Watch how water quality affects growth and survival

Respond to increasing pollution and predators over time

The goal isn’t to “win,” but to explore how oysters act as ecosystem engineers, and how environmental stressors can limit recovery when reefs are too small or degraded.
